
COLOUR - Changes the FLOS colour scheme

Usage: COLOUR pen [pap] [bord] [curs] 

pen = xy, where 'x' is the character's
background colour and 'y' is the
foreground colour from the following
table:
             
0 - transparent 8 - white
1 - black       9 - dark grey
2 - blue        a - mid grey
3 - red         b - light grey
4 - magenta     c - orange
5 - green       d - light blue
6 - cyan        e - light green
7 - yellow      f - brown

[Pap], [bord] and [curs] are 12 bit
$RGB values for the paper, border
and cursor colours.
